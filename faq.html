Frequently Asked Questions

How do I track my order?
Head to My Orders in your account. Every active order shows a live tracking button that opens a real-time map.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept Visa, Mastercard, Amex, UPI, PayPal, Apple Pay, Google Pay, all major wallets, net banking, and cash on delivery.
What is your return policy?
30 days, no questions asked. Just open the order and hit "Return". We arrange pickup at no cost.
How long does shipping take?
2–4 business days on standard shipping, next-day with Express, same-day in select metros.
Is my payment information secure?
Yes. We use 256-bit SSL encryption and are PCI-DSS Level 1 certified. We never store your CVV.
Can I cancel my order?
Orders can be cancelled for free until they enter the "Shipped" state. Go to Orders → Cancel.
Do you ship internationally?
Yes, to 40+ countries. Duties and taxes shown at checkout — no surprises.
How do I earn NovaCart rewards?
Every $1 spent = 10 points. 1000 points = $10 off your next order. Tier bonuses at Silver, Gold, and Platinum levels.
